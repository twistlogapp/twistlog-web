2. Not a Medical Device — Critical Limitations

What TwistLog does

TwistLog detects the physical opening of a prescription bottle cap using a gyroscope sensor embedded in the TwistLog ring. When a twist is detected, a timestamp is logged. That is all the hardware does.

What TwistLog does not do

  • TwistLog does not verify that medication was removed from the bottle.
  • TwistLog does not verify that medication was swallowed or ingested.
  • TwistLog does not verify the correct dosage was taken.
  • TwistLog does not provide drug interaction checks or clinical guidance.
  • TwistLog does not connect to pharmacy systems, medical records, or insurance providers.
  • TwistLog does not guarantee delivery of reminders, notifications, or alerts.
