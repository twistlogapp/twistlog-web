3. What We Collect

What we collect

  • Account information: your email address and any name you provide when creating an account.
  • Medication names: the names of medications you enter into the app. These are user-entered and self-reported — we do not connect to pharmacy systems or medical records.
  • Bottle open events: timestamps of when the TwistLog sensor ring detects a bottle cap being twisted open. These are transmitted via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to your phone and synced to our servers.
  • Device identifiers: your phone model, operating system version, and a unique device identifier used to associate your ring with your account.
  • App usage data: basic interaction logs used to identify and fix bugs (e.g., which screens were visited, crash reports).
  • Communications: if you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence.

What we do NOT collect

  • Whether you actually ingested medication (we only detect bottle openings)
  • Dosage amounts or pill counts
  • Pharmacy records, prescriptions, or insurance information
  • Medical conditions, diagnoses, or health history
  • Social Security Number or government ID
  • Financial or payment information (handled by Apple / Stripe — not stored by us)
  • Location data beyond your IP address
  • Contact lists or calendar data

Sensor and BLE Data

The TwistLog ring stores a small event buffer locally on the device. When your phone is in Bluetooth range, this buffer is transmitted to the app and synced to our servers. If the ring is lost or stolen, locally buffered events that have not yet synced may be accessible to someone with a BLE scanner. We recommend deleting your ring from the app if it is lost.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the Service: syncing dose events, sending reminders, displaying dose history.
  • To send you notifications you have requested, including Apple Watch haptic reminders and double-dose alerts.
  • To improve the Service: analyzing aggregate usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features. This analysis uses de-identified, aggregated data only.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and launch announcements (if you signed up for the waitlist).
  • To comply with applicable law.

We do not use your medication data for marketing. We do not use your data to train AI models or sell insights to third parties.

5. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your medication names, dose timestamps, or account information with pharmaceutical companies, advertisers, data brokers, research institutions, or insurance companies.

Limited sharing occurs only in these situations

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and Apple/Google for push notifications. These providers access data only to perform services for us and are contractually prohibited from using it for other purposes.
  • Caregiver access (optional, user-controlled): If you choose to share your dose history with a caregiver through the app, that caregiver can view the data you have explicitly shared. You can revoke this access at any time.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfer: If PivotEdge LLC is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance and you will have the option to delete your account before the transfer occurs.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and encryption at rest for all stored data. Access to production data is limited to authorized personnel and requires multi-factor authentication.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your account, we will notify you at your registered email address within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your medication event data and account information for 24 months from the date of collection, or until you delete your account, whichever comes first.

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days. Aggregate, de-identified data derived from your usage may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you.

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app, or email us at hello@twistlog.com.
